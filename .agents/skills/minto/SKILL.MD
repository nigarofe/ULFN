- Where the requirements below are not met in the text selected by the user, the model shall suggest how to fix the text to meet the requirements.
- The model shall create a new version of the text that meets the requirements and write it to the file just below the text selected by the user, with ´**Suggested new version**´

- The text shall not be in first person.

<!-- p53 -->
- The model shall suggest induction rather than deduction to formulate the argument on the Key Line level.

<!-- p56, p68 -->
- The paragraphs shall be structured as one of the following four types:

### SCQA
Situation - Complication - Question - Answer
- Have a task to perform - Something stops us from performing that task - What should we do? - Answer
- Have a problem - Know the solution - How do we implement the solution? - Answer
- Have a problem - A solution has been suggested - Is it the right solution? - Answer
- Took an action - Action didn't work - Why not? - Answer
