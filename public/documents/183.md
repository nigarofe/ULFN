# Proposition
Select all of the exact differential equations:

- [x] $y^2 + 2xy \frac{dy}{dx} = 0$
- [ ] $y + xy \frac{dy}{dx} = 0$
- [x] $5y + 10x + 5x \frac{dy}{dx} = 0$
- [x] $-2y -2x \frac{dy}{dx} = 0$
- [ ] $\frac{1}{y^2} - \frac{x}{y^2} \frac{dy}{dx} = 0$
- [x] $xy + \frac{x^2}{2} \frac{dy}{dx} = 0$
- [ ] $\frac{1}{y^2} - \frac{3x}{y^3} \frac{dy}{dx} = 0$
- [ ] $y^2 + 1 + xy \frac{dy}{dx} = 0$

# Step-by-step
## a
$$
y^2 + 2xy \frac{dy}{dx} = 0
$$
$$
M(x,y) + N(x,y) \frac{dy}{dx} = 0
$$
$$
M(x,y) = y^2 \quad N(x,y) = 2xy
$$
$$
\frac{\partial M}{\partial y} \stackrel{?}{=} \frac{\partial N}{\partial x}
$$
$$
2y \stackrel{\checkmark}{=} 2y
$$
