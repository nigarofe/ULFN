# Proposition
Find the sum of A and B:

a) $A = \begin{pmatrix} 4 & 3 \\ 2 & 1 \end{pmatrix}$ and $B = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{pmatrix}$

b) $A = \begin{pmatrix} 9 & 9 \\ 9 & 9 \end{pmatrix}$ and $B = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{pmatrix}$

# Step-by-step

# Answers
a) $A + B = \begin{pmatrix} 5 & 5 \\ 5 & 5 \end{pmatrix}$

b) $A + B = \begin{pmatrix} 10 & 11 \\ 12 & 13 \end{pmatrix}$
