# Proposition
Solve the following differential equations:

a) $y^{(4)} - 16y = 0$, where $y(0) = 3$, $y'(0) = 2$, $y''(0) = -4$ and $y'''(0) = -24$
b) $y^{(4)} - 81y = 0$, where $y(0) = 4$, $y'(0) = 12$, $y''(0) = -18$ and $y'''(0) = -162$

Solved items:
- a
- b
- a
- b

# Step-by-step
## a
$$y^{(4)} - 16y = 0$$
$$ y(0) = 3 \quad y'(0) = 2 \quad  y''(0) = -4 \quad y'''(0) = -24$$
$$r^4 - 16 = 0$$
$$r = \sqrt[4]{16} = 2, -2, 2i, -2i$$
$$y(x) = c_1 e^{r_1x} + c_2 e^{r_2x} + c_3 \sin nx + c_4 \cos nx$$
$$y(x) = c_1 e^{2x} + c_2 e^{-2x} + c_3 \sin 2x + c_4 \cos 2x$$
$$y(0) = c_1 + c_2 + c_4 = 3$$
$$y'(x) = 2c_1 e^{2x} - 2c_2 e^{-2x} + 2c_3 \cos 2x - 2c_4 \sin 2x$$
$$y'(0) = 2c_1 - 2c_2 + 2c_3 = 2$$
$$y''(x) = 4c_1 e^{2x} + 4c_2 e^{-2x} - 4c_3 \sin 2x - 4c_4 \cos 2x$$
$$y''(0) = 4c_1 + 4c_2 - 4c_4 = -4$$
$$y'''(x) = 8c_1 e^{2x} - 8c_2 e^{-2x} - 8c_3 \cos 2x + 8c_4 \sin 2x$$
$$y'''(0) = 8c_1 - 8c_2 - 8c_3 = -24$$

$$\left[
\begin{array}{cccc|r}
    1 & 1 & 0 & 1 & 3 \\
    2 & -2 & 2 & 0 &    2 \\
    4 & 4 & 0 & -4 & -4 \\
    8 & -8 & -8 & 0 & -24 \\
\end{array}
\right]
$$

$$\implies c_1 = 0, c_2 = 1, c_3 = 2, c_4 = 2$$

$$y(x) = e^{-2x} + 2 \cos 2x + 2 \sin 2x$$

## b
$$\quad y^{(4)} - 81y = 0 \quad y(0)=4 \quad y'(0)=12 \quad y''(0)=-18 \quad y'''(0)=-162$$

$$r^4 - 81 = 0$$

$$r^4 = 81$$

$$r = \pm \sqrt[4]{81} = -3, 3, 3i, -3i \Rightarrow y = C_1 e^{-3x} + C_2 e^{3x} + C_3 \sin(3x) + C_4 \cos(3x)$$

$$y(0) = C_1 + C_2 + C_4 = 4$$

$$y' = -3 C_1 e^{-3x} + 3 C_2 e^{3x} + 3 C_3 \cos(3x) - 3 C_4 \sin(3x)$$

$$y'' = 9 C_1 e^{-3x} + 9 C_2 e^{3x} - 9 C_3 \sin(3x) - 9 C_4 \cos(3x)$$

$$y''' = -27 C_1 e^{-3x} + 27 C_2 e^{3x} - 27 C_3 \cos(3x) + 27 C_4 \sin(3x)$$

$$y'(0) = -3 C_1 + 3 C_2 + 3 C_3 = 12$$

$$y''(0) = 9 C_1 + 9 C_2 - 9 C_4 = -18$$

$$y'''(0) = -27 C_1 + 27 C_2 - 27 C_3 = -162$$

$$
\left[ \begin{array}{cccc|c}
1 & 1 & 0 & 1 & 4 \cr
-3 & 3 & 3 & 0 & 12 \cr
9 & 9 & 0 & -9 & -18 \cr
-27 & 27 & -27 & 0 & -162
\end{array} \right]
$$

$$y = e^{-3x} + 5 \sin(3x) + 3 \cos(3x)$$

# Answer

$$\boxed{y = e^{-2x} + 2 \cos 2x + 2 \sin 2x}$$

## b
