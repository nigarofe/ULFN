# Proposition
Solve these differential equations by separating them:

a) $\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{2x^4 + 2y^4}{xy^3}$
b) $\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{x^2 + 2y^2}{xy}$
c) $\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{x^3 + 2y^3}{xy^2}$
d) $\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{5x^5 + 2y^5}{xy^4}$

Solved items:
- a
- b
- c
- a


# Step-by-step
## a - Method f(x,y) = f(tx, ty)
$$
\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{2x^4 + 2y^4}{xy^3}
$$
$$
f(x,y) \stackrel{?}{=} f(tx, ty)
$$
$$
\frac{2x^4 + 2y^4}{xy^3} \rightarrow \frac{2(tx)^4 + 2(ty)^4}{(tx)(ty)^3} = \frac{2t^4x^4 + 2t^4y^4}{txt^3y^3} = \cancel{\frac{t^4}{t^4}} \, \frac{2x^4 + 2y^4}{xy^3}
$$
$$
f(x,y) \stackrel{\checkmark}{=} f(tx, ty) \implies y = vx
$$
$$
\frac{dvx}{dx} = \frac{2x^4 + 2(vx)^4}{x(vx)^3} = \cancel{\frac{x^4}{x^4}} \, \frac{2 + 2v^4}{v^3}
$$

---

$$
\frac{d}{dx} fg = fg' + f'g \implies \frac{d}{dx} vx = vx' + v'x = v \frac{dx}{dx} + x \frac{dv}{dx} = v + x \frac{dv}{dx}
$$

---

$$
v + x \frac{dv}{dx} = \frac{2 + 2v^4}{v^3}
$$
$$
x \frac{dv}{dx} = \frac{2 + 2v^4}{v^3} - v
$$
$$
x \frac{dv}{dx} = \frac{2 + 2v^4}{v^3} - \frac{v^4}{v^3}
$$
$$
x \frac{dv}{dx} = \frac{2 + v^4}{v^3}
$$
$$
\frac{1}{x} \frac{dx}{dv} = \frac{v^3}{2 + v^4}
$$
$$
\frac{1}{x} \frac{dx}{dv} = \frac{v^3}{2 + v^4}
$$
$$
\frac{1}{x} dx = \frac{v^3}{2 + v^4} dv
$$
$$
\int \frac{1}{x} dx = \int \frac{v^3}{2 + v^4} dv
$$

---

$$
\int \frac{v^3}{2 + v^4} dv
$$
$$
u = 2 + v^4 \implies \frac{du}{dv} = 4v^3 \implies dv = \frac{1}{4v^3} du
$$
$$
\int \frac{\cancel{v^3}}{u} \cdot \frac{1}{4\cancel{v^3}} du = \frac{1}{4} \int \frac{1}{u} du = \frac{1}{4} \ln|u| + C = \frac{1}{4} \ln|2 + v^4| + C
$$

---

$$
\ln |x| = \frac{1}{4} \ln|2 + v^4| + C
$$
$$
4 \ln |x| = \ln|2 + v^4| + C
$$
$$
\ln (x^4) = \ln(2 + v^4) + C
$$
$$
e^{\ln (x^4)} = e^{\ln(2 + v^4) + C}
$$
$$
x^4 = e^{\ln(2 + v^4)} \cdot e^C
$$
$$
x^4 = (2 + v^4) \cdot C
$$
$$
\frac{x^4}{C} -2 = v^4
$$
$$
y = vx \implies v = \frac{y}{x}
$$
$$
\left(\frac{y}{x}\right)^4 = \frac{x^4}{C} - 2
$$
$$
y^4 = \frac{x^8}{C} - 2x^4
$$
$$
y = (Cx^8 - 2x^4)^{1/4}
$$



# Answer
a) $y = (Cx^8 - 2x^4)^{1/4}$
b) $y = (Cx^4 - x^2)^{1/2}$
c) $y = (Cx^6 - x^3)^{1/3}$
d) $y = (Cx^{10} - 5x^5)^{1/5}$
