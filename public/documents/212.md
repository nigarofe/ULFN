# Proposition
Find the product of A and B:

a) $A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}$ and $B = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 2 \\ 2 & 2 \end{pmatrix}$

b) $A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{pmatrix}$ and $B = \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ 7 \end{pmatrix}$

# Step-by-step
- [MACETE - Multiplicação de matrizes](https://youtu.be/8FwQM4H04kY?si=3ApIZLHyr3nUhY9P)

# Answers
a) $A \cdot B = \begin{pmatrix} 4 & 4 \\ 4 & 4 \end{pmatrix}$

b) $A \cdot B = \begin{pmatrix} 19 \\ 43 \end{pmatrix}$
