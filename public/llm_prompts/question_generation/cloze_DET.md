

- The exercise shall be about [subject]

- The exercise classification of **Cloze** shall be defined as exercises which require filling in blanks or partial answers
- The exercise shall have two paragraphs

- The text inside the braces and asterisks shall be treated as the possible correct answers
- The possible correct answers for each blank shall be separated by the pipe character `|`
example
```markdown
The quick brown **{fox}** jumps over the **{lazy|sluggish}** dog
```

- The output shall be in a Markdown code block
- The latex math syntax shall be used when needed ($...$ for inline, $$...$$ for block)
- The beginning of the output shall be:
```markdown
---
Exercise Classification	:	Cloze
Discipline				:	Duolingo English Test
Source					:   Gemini
Description				:   [fill description here]
---

## Exercise about [fill title here]
```
